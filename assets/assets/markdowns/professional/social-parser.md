## Problem
Client required real-time, precise, and accurate feedback of their product from social media.

## Solution
1. **Selenium** powered social media parser.
2. **BERT**-based
  - **Named Entity Recognition** (NER) model: Finds out the **Who**, **what**, **why**.
  - **Sentiment classifier** model: Finds out **likes** or **dislikes**.

## Result
Client reported **10% lower churn** and **15% improved sentiment** after targeted bug-fixes and marketing campaigns.
