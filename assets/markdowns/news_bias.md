## Problem Statement

Media bias is difficult to quantify consistently across countries and outlets.

## Details

- Fine-tuned LLaMA-2 using QLoRA to score sentiment and stance in news articles.

- Applied BERTopic for unsupervised topic modeling across international datasets.

- Designed statistical tests to compare local vs international reporting by country.

- Found statistically significant sentiment and stance bias in Chinese media; no significant bias detected in Canadian media.
